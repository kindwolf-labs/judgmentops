Hello Cohere enterprise AI / developer ecosystem / solutions team,

I maintain JudgmentOps, an early open-source judgment layer for making long-running enterprise AI workflows auditable, repeatable, and reviewable.

Enterprise AI can produce useful output while still losing the original business goal, constraints, acceptance standard, public/private boundaries, or lessons from prior failures across a long workflow. JudgmentOps complements existing models, retrieval systems, and agents with intent contracts, quality and redaction gates, failure memory, independent review, and human approval checkpoints.

Cohere is a strong fit because its platform emphasizes secure enterprise AI, private deployment, retrieval, workflow automation, and audit-ready visibility. The proposed next phase would run public paired evaluations comparing ordinary Cohere-powered workflows with the same workflows under protocol governance, including retrieval-grounded failure-memory cases.

The repository is an early public prototype, not a finished product, compliance solution, or proven benchmark.
